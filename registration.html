Registration & Account

I was tasked to create prototypes for a registration and account application for client-facing use. This application includes information intake about the agent, billing information, and a dashboard for all their current and past orders. Future iterations of this account application will include a notification system and more robust order status dashboard. This application will be used with our internal system as a part of the larger ordering system.

 

ROLES
— Concept & Strategy
— Art Direction
— UX/UI Design

Signup & Login

Users will be taken to this login platform where they are able to log back into their account or sign up for a new account.

Desktop View

The account page includes a dashboard, my orders, account and billing section. The ordering status system is the highlight of this application, agents will be able to track and see the status of their order. The status of their order is broken down by color and can be filtered by order date and delivery date.
